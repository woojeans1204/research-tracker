# Topic Rules

## Scope
- Track recent LLM reasoning papers and closely related work.
- Include topics such as chain-of-thought, inference-time scaling, test-time compute, planning, search, verification, self-correction, tool-using reasoning, and reasoning benchmarks.

## Inclusion Rules
- Prefer papers from the last 6 months.
- Prefer papers with strong experimental quality, meaningful baselines, and clear validation signals.
- Prefer work that is centrally about reasoning, not only loosely adjacent.

## Exclusion Rules
- Do not add papers older than the active recency window unless explicitly requested.
- Do not prioritize hype over evidence quality.
- Do not log papers that are only weakly related to reasoning without noting the reason.

## Preferred Topic Tags
- reasoning
- chain-of-thought
- inference-time-scaling
- test-time-compute
- planning
- search
- verification
- self-correction
- tool-use
- reasoning-benchmarks

## Workflow Notes
- Add unread candidates to `reading-queue.md`.
- Move completed or skipped papers to `paper-log.md`.
- Update `Last updated` whenever a file changes.
- Keep entries compact and consistent.
